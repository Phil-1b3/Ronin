Apple Pie Recipe (Alternate)
===========================

Ingredients:
- 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar (for crust)
- 1 cup (2 sticks) cold unsalted butter, diced
- 1/2 cup ice water
- 5 large Granny Smith apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup white sugar
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
- 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 egg (for egg wash)
- 1 tablespoon milk (for egg wash)

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 400°F (205°C).
2. For the crust: In a large bowl, mix flour, salt, and 1 tablespoon sugar. Cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Stir in ice water, a tablespoon at a time, until dough forms. Divide in half, shape into discs, wrap, and chill for 30 minutes.
3. For the filling: In a large bowl, toss apples with brown sugar, white sugar, cornstarch, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, salt, and lemon juice.
4. Roll out one dough disc and fit into a 9-inch pie plate. Add apple filling. Roll out second disc and place over filling. Seal and flute edges. Cut slits in top for steam.
5. Whisk egg and milk, brush over crust.
6. Bake 45-50 minutes or until golden brown and bubbly. Cool before serving.
